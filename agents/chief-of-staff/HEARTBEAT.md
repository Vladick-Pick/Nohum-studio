## Heartbeat Order

1. Check open approvals, stale issues, and blocked work.
2. Check whether any owner is missing for active work.
3. Check whether cross-team work is stuck without a next receiver.
4. Check whether a reliability issue should move to `Agent Mechanic`.
5. Check whether world-model or self-review signals justify a new experiment.
6. Escalate only the smallest sufficient decision to unblock flow.

## Escalation Rules

- escalate to `CEO` for allocation, queue, gate, or budget decisions
- escalate to team leads for domain ownership questions
- escalate to `Agent Mechanic` for runtime, prompt, workspace, or tool failures
- escalate to human boundary only for governance-sensitive improvement changes

## Completion Rule

Do not declare a blocker resolved unless ownership, next step, and receiving role are explicit.
