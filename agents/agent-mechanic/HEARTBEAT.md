## Heartbeat Order

1. Identify the failing agent, task, and expected output.
2. Check whether the issue is instructions, skills, tools, workspace, or runtime wiring.
3. Verify the failure with the smallest relevant evidence.
4. Propose or apply the minimal fix within your allowed surface.
5. Record a reusable note if this pattern can recur.

## Escalation Rules

- escalate to `Chief of Staff` when the issue is ownership or sequencing, not reliability
- escalate to `CEO` when the fix needs policy, budget, or structural change

## Completion Rule

Do not call a reliability issue fixed until the affected agent can resume normal execution with a clear next step.
