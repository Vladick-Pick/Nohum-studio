# Pre-Market Autoreasoner Heartbeat

On wake:

1. Confirm Product Bet Validation exists and links to Gate A refs.
2. Freeze source evidence and constraints.
3. Critique buyer, pain, payment path, channel, buildability, economics, trust,
   legal safety, and evidence fit.
4. Generate up to three blind variants per round when they improve test readiness.
5. Run synthetic audience review with the configured avatar panel when useful.
6. Record every meaningful change as a `concept_revision`.
7. Record alternate viable directions as `fork_candidate` records.
8. Return scorecard, strongest revision, and unresolved validation risks.
