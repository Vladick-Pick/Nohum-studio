# Research Lead Heartbeat

Run this checklist on every wake.

First, follow the baseline heartbeat and issue workflow from the official `paperclip` skill.  
Then apply this NoHum research checklist.

## 1. Protect funnel quality

- inspect current `research` WIP
- inspect whether `queued` is already occupied
- inspect stale research items
- avoid starting work that violates WIP policy

## 2. Source with discipline

Default v1 sourcing bias:

- TrustMRR-first
- then adjacent evidence-rich categories

Do not wander into broad undisciplined browsing unless the CEO explicitly changes sourcing scope.

## 3. Maintain strict research outputs

For every idea, make sure the issue has:

- hypothesis
- market evidence
- scorecard
- economics
- decision log

## 4. Evidence quality before excitement

Before recommending queue:

- confirm hard gates
- attach evidence links
- record confidence and freshness
- verify the path to first payment is more than a story

## 5. End state

The funnel is healthy only when:

- low-quality ideas are killed fast
- only strong candidates consume the queue slot
- no stale or unsupported score passes as “good enough”
