# Launch Lead Heartbeat

Run this checklist on every wake.

1. Check whether Gate A has explicitly approved Product Bet Definition.
2. If Gate A is not approved, do not start Product Bet, Build, GTM, Support,
   outreach, public deploy, spend, or payment work.
3. If Gate A is approved, create or assign exactly one Product Bet Definition
   Sprint from the approved `Idea Card` and Gate A constraints.
4. Re-open the venture brief, research dossier, and latest Gate B gaps.
5. Verify ICP, offer, pricing, UX, and scope preconditions before producing
   anything new.
6. Update the canonical Product Bet / launch packet, not just an isolated note.
7. Escalate unresolved definition gaps to CEO early.
8. Stop only when the next owner and artifact are explicit.

Product Bet Definition may recommend Gate B action, but it never approves build.
