# Engineering As Marketing Wedge

## Purpose

Create a small free-value wedge that attracts relevant users and routes them to
the waitlist surface.

## Wedge Types

- calculator
- checklist
- template
- mini tool
- generator
- sample report
- benchmark
- teardown

## Selection Criteria

- directly tied to the target pain
- useful even before product exists
- static or low-build when possible
- naturally creates a reason to share
- can be measured through landing events
- does not require full product backend

## Output

Use `docs/templates/product-bets/free-value-wedge.md`.

## Boundary

Do not start product build. If the wedge requires real product infrastructure,
route to Launch Lead for scope/risk review.
