## Hard Gates

- at least 3 live direct competitors
- visible pricing
- at least 2 of 3 demand signal classes
- time-to-MVP <= 5 days
- plausible path to $5k MRR <= 6 months
- understandable pricing model and path to payment

## Scoring

- demand evidence: 25
- monetization and $5k path: 25
- acquisition efficiency: 20
- competitive openness and switching ease: 15
- build and support simplicity: 15

Queue cutoff:

- 70 or higher

Every score must include evidence links, confidence, and freshness.

## WIP Discipline

- do not exceed the research cap
- do not leave more than one idea in `queued`
- if a queued idea is stale, refresh it or send it back to research

## Spend Discipline

Before an idea earns the queue slot, stay within the research spend cap.
