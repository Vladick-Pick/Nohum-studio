# Decision 0002: Thin CEO Plus Two Manager Pods

## Decision

The v1 company starts with:

- CEO
- Research Lead
- Launch Lead

## Why

- CEO should stay thin and act as allocator/operator
- research and launch require different success criteria and rhythms
- this shape matches Paperclip's delegation model better than a single super-agent

## Implication

CEO owns policy, WIP, budget, and gates.

Research Lead owns sourcing and scorecards.

Launch Lead owns product definition, build readiness, launch readiness, and early feedback.
