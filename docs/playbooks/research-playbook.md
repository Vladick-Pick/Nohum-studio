# Research Playbook

Trigger: queue slot is free and research capacity exists.

Active roles:
- `CEO`
- `Research Lead`
- research specialists when present

Canonical outputs:
- `trustmrr intake card`
- `hypothesis`
- `market-evidence`
- `scorecard`
- `economics`
- `decision-log`
- `canonical queue package`

Exit condition:
- `KILL`
- `KILL FOR NOW`
- `QUEUE`

Blockers:
- missing funnel surface
- missing evidence links
- hard-gates failure
- unresolved evidence gaps
- stale or weak queue package
