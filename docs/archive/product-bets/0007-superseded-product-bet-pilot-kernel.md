# Decision 0007: Superseded Product Bet Phase 0

## Status

Superseded.

The current doctrine is:

```text
Idea Card owns market truth.
Gate A opens product definition, not build.
Product Bet owns product shape after Gate A.
Gate B owns build permission.
```

## Superseding Decision

The earlier Phase 0 approach placed product-bet-style decision work before Gate
A. That created a duplicate decision unit beside the `Idea Card`.

The replacement model keeps Research as the only pre-Gate-A proof surface:

- `Idea Card` owns market, demand, competition, monetization, doctrine, and
  final Research verdict.
- Gate A approves product definition work.
- Product Bet Definition starts after Gate A.
- Gate B approves build.

## Migration Implication

- Pre-Gate-A source intake belongs to `Idea Scout` and `Research Lead`.
- Pre-Gate-A proof belongs to `Competitor Scout`, `Demand Validator`, and
  `Revenue Validator` inside the canonical `Idea Card`.
- Product Bet agents are post-Gate-A and stay paused until Gate A opens product
  definition for a venture.
- Older Product Bet skills can be reused after Gate A if their wording and
  boundaries match the new lifecycle.
