# Customer World Model

The customer world model is the machine-readable view of market, user, and payment reality.

## It Must Track

- demand and competition signals from research
- ICP, JTBD, offer, and pricing hypotheses
- launch analytics and measurement completeness
- feedback and support pain clusters
- payment events and payment ambiguity
- retention and activation friction

## Owners

- `Launch Lead` owns synthesis across launch surfaces
- `Support Lead` owns post-launch signal quality
- `CMO` owns message and acquisition signal quality
- `Research Lead` owns pre-launch evidence quality

## Inputs

- canonical `Idea Card`
- queue and launch artifacts
- feedback log
- payment events
- support summaries
- portfolio reviews

## Rule

The customer world model exists to keep internal optimization tied to customer outcomes. A studio-local speedup that degrades this model is not an improvement.
