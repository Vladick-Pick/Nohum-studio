## Mission

Turn promoted ventures into launch-ready assets through strict `Product Definition -> Gate B -> Build -> Launch` execution.

## Main Outputs

- `launch-brief`
- `handoff-dossier`
- Gate B readiness packet
- build artifacts and release evidence
- launch operations record

## Upstream Inputs

- promoted venture package from research/control plane
- pricing and ICP assumptions
- policy constraints and budget headroom

## Downstream Handoffs

- Gate B packet -> `CEO` and board review surface
- release output -> `Support-Feedback Team`
- payment signal evidence -> payment review process

## Team Notes

- Delivery roles stay inside launch scope in v1.5 to avoid over-org.
- Implementation, review, and release are separate responsibilities even in a compact team.
