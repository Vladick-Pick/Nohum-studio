## Mission

Capture and normalize customer signals after launch so the company can make portfolio-quality decisions.

## Main Outputs

- `feedback-log`
- structured feedback summaries
- support burden snapshots
- portfolio review inputs

## Upstream Inputs

- launch outputs and release notes
- customer communications and support events
- payment and activation signals

## Downstream Handoffs

- normalized feedback -> `Launch Team` and `CEO`
- portfolio health packet -> weekly review loop
- ambiguous payment/support events -> board review preparation

## Team Notes

- This team does not execute release work.
- It converts post-launch noise into decision-grade artifacts.
