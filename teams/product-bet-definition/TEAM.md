## Manager

`Launch Lead`

## Member Agents

- `Product Bet Compiler`
- `Competitor Deep Dive Analyst`
- `Economics Modeler`
- `Offer And Positioning Strategist`
- `Organic Traffic Strategist`
- `Pre-Market Autoreasoner`
- `Landing Surface Builder`
- `Product Bet Measurement Specialist`
- `Evidence Router`

## Mission

Turn a Gate-A-approved Idea Card into versioned Product Bet revisions, a
landing/waitlist surface, organic traffic attempts, observation-window
evidence, and a Gate B recommendation without starting build.

## Activation Rule

This team is imported with the company, but it is dormant until CEO records a
Gate A decision and creates `Run Product Bet Validation Sprint`.

Agents must not self-start from ambient context. Launch Lead opens the shared
Product Bet Card and creates specialist assignments.

## Main Outputs

- Product Bet Card
- competitor deep dive pack
- financial model
- offer brief
- organic traffic strategy
- pain language map, search intent map, community prospecting map
- autoreason report
- concept revisions and fork candidates
- selected test revision
- landing design, copy variants, waitlist form, surface version, surface QA
- validation risk map
- test GTM surface pack
- measurement plan
- observation window
- traffic attempts and traffic source report
- evidence events
- validation cycle report
- Gate B recommendation

## Boundaries

- Gate A opens product definition, not build.
- Product Bet Validation does not rewrite the frozen Idea Card.
- Gate B recommendation is not Gate B approval.
- Agents may only run validation actions allowed by the Gate A constraints and
  linked approval refs.
